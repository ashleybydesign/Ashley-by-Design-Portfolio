Travel Genie

The creation of the Travel Genie platform involved a comprehensive and user-centric approach to address the challenges faced by modern travelers. The goal was to streamline the travel research, planning, and booking processes into a single, user-friendly platform. The process began with a thorough analysis of the problem at hand: the fragmentation of travel resources across various digital platforms. This formed the basis of the problem statement. Travel Genie aimed to simplify this experience by providing a consolidated hub for all travel-related information and solutions.
